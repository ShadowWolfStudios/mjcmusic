About MJC Music

Experienced performer and aspiring teacher

I combine music education with live performance expertise to help students grow technically, creatively, and confidently.

Lessons are tailored around your goals—whether you're preparing for auditions, building stage presence, or exploring original music.

Teaching Philosophy

Every lesson is structured to keep progress clear, fun, and meaningful. Growth happens through consistent practice, strong support, and real performance opportunities.

I work closely with students to set goals, choose repertoire, and build the skills they need for success.

Music should always feel like a reward, never a chore. My goal is to encourage musicians to keep playing throughout their lives.

I have been a musician for about 10 years now. Between school and community, I have developed a passion for both performance and education.
